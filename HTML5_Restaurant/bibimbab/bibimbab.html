Bibimbab Description

In our Restaurant, we cook Bibimbab with 6 different vegetables, fried egg, and small portion of Bulgogi. We also serve Bibimbab with a small Miso soup, and 5 different side dishes. Customers also can order vegetarian option for Bibimbab.

Bibimbab Ingredients

  • Red pepper paste and Sesame oil
  • Seasoned spinach, bean sprouts, shiitake mushroom, and carrots
  • Small portion of Bulgogi
  • One fried egg
